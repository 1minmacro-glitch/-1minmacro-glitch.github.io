wallst-bot — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026  |  Last Updated: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how wallst-bot ("we", "our", or "the Application") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our application that connects to the TikTok API.

1. Information We Collect

wallst-bot is a personal automation tool used solely by its owner to upload financial content videos to TikTok. When authorizing the application via TikTok OAuth 2.0, the following information is accessed:

  • TikTok Account Basic Information (scope: user.info.basic): open_id, union_id, display name, avatar URL — used solely to confirm account identity.
  • Video Upload Permission (scope: video.upload): used to programmatically upload video content to the authorized TikTok account.

We do not collect personal data from any third-party users. No end-user registration or login is involved.

2. How We Use the Information

The information accessed through TikTok API is used exclusively for the following purpose:

  • Authenticating the app owner's TikTok account to enable automated video uploads.
  • Verifying that uploads were successfully submitted to TikTok.

The data is never used for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any commercial purpose beyond the stated functionality.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally on the app owner's device only (in a local file: tiktok_token_wallst.json).
  • No data is transmitted to external servers other than TikTok's official API endpoints.
  • Tokens are protected by the operating system's file access controls.
  • We implement reasonable security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Data Retention

  • OAuth tokens are retained locally until manually deleted or until the TikTok authorization is revoked.
  • No personal data is retained beyond what TikTok's API requires for authentication.
  • You may revoke the application's access at any time via TikTok's account settings at tiktok.com/settings/connected-apps.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any data with third parties. The only data transfer that occurs is between the application and TikTok's official API endpoints, as required for the service to function.

We may disclose information if required by law or legal process.

6. Third-Party Services

wallst-bot uses the TikTok Content Posting API. By using this application, you acknowledge that TikTok's data practices are governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy, available at:
https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

7. User Rights

As the sole user of this application, the app owner retains full control over all data. You have the right to:

  • Access: Review stored tokens by inspecting local files.
  • Delete: Remove all stored data by deleting local token files.
  • Revoke: Revoke TikTok authorization at any time via TikTok account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

wallst-bot is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. International Data Transfers

Authentication data is processed by TikTok's servers, which may be located outside your country of residence. Such transfers are governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
