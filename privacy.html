This Privacy Policy explains how 1MinMacro ("we", "our", or "the Service") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our content publishing platform, including features that connect to the TikTok API.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to 1MinMacro via TikTok's OAuth 2.0 authorization, the following information is accessed:

  • TikTok Account Basic Information (scope: user.info.basic): open_id, union_id, display name, avatar URL — used to identify and display your connected account.
  • Video Upload Permission (scope: video.upload): used to publish video content to your authorized TikTok account on your behalf.

We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide the Service.

2. How We Use the Information

Information accessed through the TikTok API is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating your TikTok account to enable authorized video uploads.
  • Publishing content to your TikTok account as directed by you.
  • Verifying that content was successfully submitted to TikTok.
  • Displaying your account information within the Service interface.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the stated functionality.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored securely on the authorized device or server.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers other than TikTok's official API endpoints.
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Retention

  • OAuth tokens are retained until you revoke authorization or request deletion.
  • You may revoke the application's access at any time via TikTok's account settings at tiktok.com/settings/connected-apps.
  • Upon account deletion or revocation, all associated token data is permanently removed.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Data is only transmitted to TikTok's official API endpoints as required to provide the Service.

We may disclose information if required by applicable law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
