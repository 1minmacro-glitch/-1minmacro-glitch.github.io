About 1MinMacro

1MinMacro is a content creation and publishing platform that helps creators automatically generate and distribute short-form video content across social media platforms including TikTok. Our service provides creators with tools to streamline their content workflow and reach a wider audience efficiently.

What We Do

We enable content creators to publish videos to TikTok using the official TikTok Content Posting API. Our platform handles video scheduling, metadata management, and publication on behalf of authorized users who have explicitly granted permission through TikTok's OAuth 2.0 authorization flow.
